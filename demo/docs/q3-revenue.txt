Q3 2026 Revenue Report

Total revenue for Q3 reached $4.2M ARR, representing 18% quarter-over-quarter growth. Enterprise segment contributed $2.8M (67% of total), while SMB accounted for $1.4M with a 24% growth rate.

Net revenue retention improved to 118%, driven by successful upsell of premium analytics features. Gross margin held steady at 78%, above the 75% target set in the Series A board deck.

Monthly recurring revenue crossed the $350K milestone in September, with 42 new enterprise logos added during the quarter. Average contract value increased 12% to $67K annually.
